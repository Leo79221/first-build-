Biography

Vegeta is the Prince of an elite warrior race known as the Saiyans and born on Planet Vegeta. The Emperor Freiza took control of the Saiyan race, Vegeta serves in Frieza force. So Vegeta commits many evil acts, but secretly longs for revenge against Frieza. Vegeta tried to take over planet earth but there was another saiyan protecting them and Vegeta lost. The Frieza force go to this planet called Planet namek to get the dragon balls so frieza can wish himself to be immortal but Vegeta steals them and hides them so he can use them himself and wish himself to be immortal. Frieza kills Vegeta and the Saiyan from earth Goku come save the day. As Goku and Frieza were fighting one of the Namekians dende wished to have every one who died to be brought back to life and transported back to earth. Vegeta stayed at Earth and rised a family there He protected Earth from terrfying foes..
